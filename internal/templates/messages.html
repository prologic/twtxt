Private Messaging

Private messaging on {{ .InstanceName }} is very simple and follows a similar form to Twt(s) themselves. In other words you can send private messages to users on the same pod as you.

Find the username of the person you want to message, and type in a short message (limited to {{ .MaxTwtLength }} characters) and hit the "Send" button.

NOTE: Messages are not encrypted or signed at this time so it is currently possible for the Pod Operator of {{ .InstanceName }} to view your private messages. This will change in time as we continue to develop this feature out and add e2e encryption. THank you.
